About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Renmin University of China (RUC), supervised by Asst. Prof. Cheng Meng. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from Tsinghua University in 2022.

My research interests include Big Data Subsampling, Optimal Transport, and Image Anomaly Detection. I am passionate about solving real-world industrial challenges using statistical methods.
